# About Pronto

**Pronto** is a minimalistic Capoeira progress tracking application designed to help practitioners organize and track their training journey through hierarchical task lists and structured training plans.

- Organizing your goals in a clear, hierarchical structure

### Tips & Best Practices

#### For Beginners

- Start with a predefined plan to understand the structure
- Don't create too many levels at once (3-4 levels is usually enough)
- Check off tasks regularly to stay motivated
- Break down intimidating goals into tiny, achievable steps

#### For Advanced Users

- Create custom plans for specific events (batizado, roda, etc.)
- Export your plans to share with your group
- Use drag & drop to reorganize as your priorities change
- Combine multiple training aspects (physical, musical, cultural)

#### Training Plan Structure

A good training plan includes:

1. **Physical Conditioning**
   - Strength exercises
   - Flexibility work

## ü§î FAQ

**Q: Can I use this for other martial arts?**
A: Absolutely! While designed for Capoeira, the hierarchical task system works for any training discipline.

**Q: Is my data private?**
A: Yes! All data is stored locally in your browser's IndexedDB. Nothing is sent to any server.

**Q: Can I sync across devices?**
A: Not currently. Export your plans as JSON and import them on other devices as a workaround.

**Q: What happens if I clear browser data?**
A: Your tasks and custom plans will be lost. Export important plans regularly as backups.

**Q: Can I print my training plan?**
A: Use your browser's print function (Ctrl/Cmd + P) to print the current view.
